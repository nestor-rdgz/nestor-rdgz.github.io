Dr. Rodriguez-Perez is an Industrial Engineer from ICAI (Comillas Pontifical University), holds a PhD in Engineering Systems Modelling from the same university, and also has a Master's Degree in Smart Grids from the University of Strathclyde (Scotland) and Comillas Pontifical University. He is currently a professor in the Department of Industrial Management at ICAI and a researcher in the REDES area at the Institute for Research in Technology. As a researcher, he has been a guest researcher at Delft University of Technology (The Netherlands) and has participated in numerous European and consultancy projects related to the digitisation of electricity networks and Smart Grids. His research interests focus on the digitisation of power systems, TSO-DSO coordination, cybersecurity for smart grids, and the replicability and scalability of information and communication technologies for smart grid solutions.
